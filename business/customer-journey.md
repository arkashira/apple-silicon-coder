# Customer Journey Map for Apple Silicon Coder

## 1. Aware
- **Trigger Event**: Announcement of new Apple Silicon hardware or performance benchmarks.
- **Friction Points**: Lack of awareness about coding models optimized for Apple Silicon; existing solutions may not be marketed effectively.
- **User Emotions**: Curiosity, excitement about new hardware capabilities, frustration over current limitations.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Educational content showcasing the benefits of optimized coding models; webinars or demos highlighting performance improvements.
- **Metric**: Number of website visits and engagement metrics on educational content (e.g., downloads, video views).

## 2. Consider
- **Trigger Event**: User research on coding models that leverage Apple Silicon's capabilities.
- **Friction Points**: Confusion over technical specifications; uncertainty about compatibility with existing tools and workflows.
- **User Emotions**: Hopefulness for improved performance, anxiety about making the right choice, skepticism about claims.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Clear comparison charts with competitors; testimonials from early adopters; free trials or demos to showcase performance.
- **Metric**: Number of demo requests and trial sign-ups.

## 3. Try
- **Trigger Event**: User signs up for a free trial or demo of the Apple Silicon Coder.
- **Friction Points**: Initial setup complexity; performance expectations may not be met if not properly configured.
- **User Emotions**: Excitement to test new capabilities, frustration if setup is challenging, relief if performance exceeds expectations.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Step-by-step onboarding guides; responsive customer support during trial; performance benchmarks to set expectations.
- **Metric**: Trial conversion rate (percentage of users who convert to paid after trial).

## 4. Adopt
- **Trigger Event**: User decides to purchase the Apple Silicon Coder after trial.
- **Friction Points**: Concerns about long-term support and updates; potential integration challenges with existing systems.
- **User Emotions**: Satisfaction from improved performance, apprehension about commitment, anticipation of future updates.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Clear communication of support and update policies; community forums for user collaboration; personalized onboarding sessions.
- **Metric**: Customer retention rate and net promoter score (NPS) post-purchase.

## 5. Expand
- **Trigger Event**: User experiences success with the Apple Silicon Coder and seeks additional features or products.
- **Friction Points**: Difficulty in finding relevant add-ons or complementary products; potential budget constraints for expansion.
- **User Emotions**: Enthusiasm for new capabilities, frustration if expansion options are unclear, loyalty if satisfied with current product.
- **Opportunities to Delight**: Bundled offers for additional features; loyalty rewards for existing customers; regular updates on new features and enhancements.
- **Metric**: Average revenue per user (ARPU) and number of users adopting additional features/products.
